## :material-information-outline: About HydroAI Lab

We study the global water cycle with deep learning and satellite remote
sensing — soil moisture, precipitation, land–atmosphere coupling.
The lab is based at **GIST** (Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology),
led by **Hyunglok Kim**. Learn more at [hydroai.net](https://hydroai.net).
