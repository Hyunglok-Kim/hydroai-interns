## :material-clipboard-check: What "done" looks like each week

=== "Week 1 — Literature scoping"
    - 5–10 papers read with one-sentence takeaways
    - Refined research question (one paragraph)
    - 10-minute Friday talk: *what's known, what's the gap, what will you try*

=== "Weeks 2–3 — Data & pipeline"
    - Datasets identified, downloaded, and inventoried (variables, resolution, period)
    - First exploratory plots (no need to be pretty — be honest about the data)
    - Reproducible script or notebook; someone else should be able to re-run it

=== "Week 4 — Mid-checkpoint"
    - Honest status: what's working, what isn't, what changed since the kickoff plan
    - Decision point — same direction? pivot? narrow scope?

=== "Weeks 5–7 — Iterate & validate"
    - Main analysis or model running end-to-end
    - At least one sensitivity / robustness test
    - Figures starting to look like *figures from a paper*, not exploratory plots

=== "Weeks 8–9 — Story & talk"
    - One-page abstract
    - Slide deck reviewed by PI
    - Final 15-min talk + 5 min Q&A
